FUN FACTS ABOUT CATS

Cats were first domesticated in the Near East around 7500 BC.
A group of cats is called a clowder
The male cat is called a (tom)

Etymology and Naming

The origin of the English word cat, is thought to be from the Late Latin word cattus. It was suggested that the word 'cattus' is derived from an Egyptian precursor "ϣⲁⲩ šau", wich translates to "tomcat"

Evolution

The domestic cat is a part of the Felidae family that had a common ancestor million years ago.The genus Felis diverged from other Felidae around 6–7 million years ago. The wild Felis species evolved through sympatric or parapatric speciation, whereas the domestic cat evolved through artificial selection.
